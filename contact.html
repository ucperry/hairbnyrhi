Visit Salon Maverick

Located in the heart of North Canton on Main Street, Salon Maverick provides a convenient and welcoming location for all your hair care needs. The salon is easily accessible with ample parking available.

I've been proudly operating from this location for over 4 years, serving clients from North Canton, Canton, Akron, and as far as Pittsburgh, PA.

Driving Directions:

  • From I-77: Take Exit 113 for Portage St, turn east toward North Canton
  • Continue on Portage St for 2 miles to Main St
  • Turn left (north) on Main St - Salon Maverick is on the right
  • Parking available in rear lot
