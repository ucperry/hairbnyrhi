Cancellation & Scheduling

Professional scheduling policies ensure fair service for all clients and protect both your time and mine.

Cancellation Policy

24-Hour Notice Required
Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will incur a cancellation fee.
  • 24+ hours notice: No charge, full refund of any deposits
  • Less than 24 hours: 50% of scheduled service cost
  • No-show: 100% of scheduled service cost
  • Same-day cancellation: 75% of scheduled service cost
Emergency Exceptions: Medical emergencies, severe weather, or family crises may be considered for fee waiver at stylist's discretion.

Rescheduling Policy

Rescheduling is encouraged over cancellation when possible.

  • First reschedule: Free with 24+ hours notice
  • Multiple reschedules: $25 fee per additional reschedule
  • Same-day reschedule: Treated as cancellation
  • Maximum reschedules: 2 per appointment before booking new consultation
Pro Tip: Book your next appointment before leaving to secure your preferred time slot.

Booking Requirements

All appointments follow a consultation-first approach to ensure quality service.

  • New clients must book consultation before first service
  • Complex color services require advance consultation
  • Appointment requests reviewed within 24 hours
  • Confirmation required before appointment is official
  • Credit card required to hold appointment

Service Policies

Quality and safety policies that ensure the best possible results for your hair.

Hair Health First

Your hair's health is the top priority. Some services may be declined to preserve hair integrity.

  • Severely damaged hair may require treatment before color services
  • Unrealistic color changes may be refused or modified
  • Alternative solutions will always be offered
  • Multiple sessions may be recommended for drastic changes
  • Final approval of all services rests with the stylist
Your hair's health matters more than any trend. Trust the professional process for best results.

Pricing & Payment

Transparent pricing with possible additional charges for complex services.

  • Base prices listed on services page
  • Additional charges for extra time or products
  • Corrective color charged at hourly rate
  • All pricing confirmed during consultation
  • Payment due at time of service
Additional Charges Apply For: Extra length/thickness, multiple colors, corrective work, overtime services, premium products upon request.

Terms & Conditions

Legal terms and conditions that govern all services and client relationships.

Service Agreement

Liability & Risk

While every precaution is taken to ensure safe and satisfactory results, hair services carry inherent risks including but not limited to:

  • Allergic reactions to chemical products
  • Unexpected color results due to previous chemical treatments
  • Hair breakage or damage from chemical processes
  • Skin sensitivity or irritation

Clients assume responsibility for disclosing all relevant hair and health history. Hair By Rhiannon is not liable for results when full disclosure is not provided.

Satisfaction Guarantee

Client satisfaction is the goal, but results may vary based on individual hair conditions and maintenance. Adjustments will be made within reason at no additional charge within 7 days of service, provided:

  • No other chemical services have been performed
  • Proper aftercare instructions were followed
  • The concern is related to the original service

Photography & Social Media

Hair By Rhiannon may request permission to photograph work for portfolio and social media use. Clients may decline without affecting service quality. Photos will not include faces unless separate consent is given.

Professional Boundaries

Hair By Rhiannon maintains professional relationships with all clients. Inappropriate behavior, harassment, or disrespectful conduct will result in immediate service termination and future service refusal.

Privacy Policy

Client information is kept confidential and secure. Personal information, service history, and payment details are protected and never shared with third parties except as required by law.
