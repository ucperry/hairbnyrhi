Meet Rhiannon

Over 20 years of passion, expertise, and dedication to making every client feel beautiful and confident.

My Journey in Hair

For over 20 years, I've had the incredible privilege of transforming not just hair, but confidence, self-expression, and the way people see themselves. What started as a passion became a calling, and that calling has led me to create lasting relationships with clients who have become like family.

My approach goes beyond just cutting and coloring hair—it's about truly understanding each person who sits in my chair. Every client has a unique story, lifestyle, and vision for themselves. My job is to listen, understand, and bring that vision to life while ensuring their hair remains healthy and beautiful.

"For me, styling is about more than just technique—it's about connection. I find so much joy in getting to know my clients and bringing their personality to life through their hair."

The beauty industry has given me the incredible gift of balance—allowing me to be both a dedicated mother to my two children and a passionate stylist. This career has never asked me to choose between my love for my craft and my love for my family, which has made every day in this industry even more meaningful.
